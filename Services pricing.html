Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services and pricing

What's included in all packages?

All packages include professional editing, high-resolution digital files with print rights, an online gallery for viewing and downloading, and personal usage rights. You'll receive your images via a private online gallery within 3-4 weeks (2 weeks for rush editing).

Do you require a deposit?

Yes, a 30% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your date. The remaining balance is due 2 weeks before your session or event. We accept credit cards, bank transfers, and payment plans for wedding packages.

What happens if we need to reschedule?

We understand life happens! You can reschedule once without penalty up to 2 weeks before your session. Additional rescheduling may incur a $100 fee. For weddings, rescheduling is subject to availability and may require a new contract.

Can we purchase additional hours or images?

Absolutely! Additional coverage hours can be added for $300/hour. Extra edited images from your session are available for $25 each. We're happy to customize any package to fit your specific needs.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! For packages over $2,000, we offer interest-free payment plans. After your deposit, remaining payments can be split into monthly installments leading up to your event date. Contact us to discuss a payment schedule that works for you.

What's your cancellation policy?

Deposits are non-refundable as they secure your date and represent lost booking opportunities. If you must cancel, we'll work with you to find a solution, such as applying your deposit to a future session or transferring to a friend or family member.

Still have questions? We're here to help!
